About HackHalt - Cyber Intelligence Council

HackHalt - CIC is a collaborative cyber initiative dedicated to building a secure and resilient digital ecosystem across India through awareness, capacity building, and actionable intelligence.

Our Mission

To combat cybercrime and build digital resilience across India through:

  • Cyber Security Training & Capacity Building
  • Security Assessment & Vulnerability Management
  • Crime Prevention & Threat Intelligence
  • Research & Analysis of emerging threats
  • Digital Forensics & Incident Response (DFIR)
  • Open Source Intelligence (OSINT)
  • Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing (VAPT)

Our Vision

A digitally secure India where individuals, enterprises, institutions, and government work collaboratively to prevent, detect, and respond to cyber threats.

We envision a future where:

  • Cyber awareness is a fundamental skill for all citizens
  • Organizations adopt security-first practices
  • Rapid incident response minimizes impact of breaches
  • Public and private sectors collaborate on cyber defense
  • Innovation in security protects our digital infrastructure

Our Core Values

Principles that guide everything we do

Collaboration

We believe in bringing together diverse stakeholders—academia, industry, government, and community—to collectively build a secure digital ecosystem.

Innovation

We continuously explore emerging technologies and methodologies to stay ahead of evolving cyber threats and provide cutting-edge solutions.

Education

We are committed to empowering individuals at all levels with practical cybersecurity knowledge and skills through accessible, engaging programs.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, ethics, and accountability in all our engagements and maintain the highest standards of professional conduct.

Impact

Our Four Pillars of Excellence

These four pillars form the foundation of everything HackHalt - CIC does, ensuring comprehensive cyber resilience across all levels of society.

Prevention

Proactive frameworks and practices that minimize cyber risk before incidents occur.

  • Security awareness campaigns
  • Policy & process consulting
  • Cyber hygiene playbooks
  • Risk assessment frameworks
  • Vulnerability management programs

Education

Structured programs that upskill students, professionals, and decision-makers in cybersecurity.

  • Capacity-building workshops
  • Curriculum design for academia
  • Corporate training modules
  • Certification programs
  • Mentorship & skill development

Detection

Building capability to detect threats, anomalies, and vulnerabilities at scale.

  • Threat hunting guidance
  • Vulnerability assessments
  • Security monitoring practices
  • OSINT & threat intelligence
  • Digital forensics analysis

Rapid Response

Incident readiness and structured response playbooks that reduce impact and recovery time.

  • Incident response playbooks
  • Post-incident reviews
  • Resilience & recovery planning
  • Business continuity frameworks
  • Crisis communication strategies

Primary Focus Areas & Engagement Model

We concentrate our efforts on key areas where we can create the most significant impact, working with diverse stakeholder groups across India.

Focus Areas

  • OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) - Gathering & analysis of publicly available information
  • DFIR (Digital Forensics & Incident Response) - Investigation and rapid incident management
  • Awareness & Hygiene - Community education on cyber safety practices
  • Governance & Compliance - Policy frameworks and regulatory guidance
  • Emerging Threats - Research on advanced persistent threats and zero-days

Stakeholder Engagement

  • Academia - Curriculum development, research collaboration
  • Industry - Consulting, training, intelligence sharing
  • Government - Policy advocacy, law enforcement partnership
  • Community - Public awareness, grassroots programs
  • Startups - Security guidance, affordable solutions

Geographic Reach

  • Pan-India Presence - Remote-first model enabling national reach
  • Regional Chapters - Localized initiatives across states
  • Urban & Rural - Inclusive approach to digital security
  • Border Security - Critical infrastructure protection
  • International Collaboration - Cross-border threat intelligence
